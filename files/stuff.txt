ACCIDENT & EMERGENCIES
This module allows swift registration of patient’s brought in and also volunteers who brought in the said patient.
It also manages the case file, lab records, prescriptions, schedule surgery,  x-ray and scan of the patient and also can transfer a patient to In-patient department if need be.

OUT PATIENT DEPARTMENT
These modules allows patient registration for patients who don’t wish to see the doctor but want to carry out tests, x-rays, scans and at some point be transferred to in patient department .

DENTISTRY
This module is derived from the doctors module.
Hence, possess all the functions in the doctors module but the test and scans requested for by the dentist are specific to his specialty.

PEDIATRICS
This module is also derived from the doctors module. This enables all the functions in the doctors module accessible by the pediatrician exempting tests and scans to be done specifically.
